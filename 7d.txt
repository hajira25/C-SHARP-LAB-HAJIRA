Abrus precatorius is commonly known as jequirity, Crab's eye, or rosary pea, paternoster pea, love pea, precatory pea or bean, prayer bead, John Crow Bead, 
Also known as coral bead, red-bead vine, country licorice Indian licorice,]wild licorice, Jamaica wild licorice, olinda (In Sri Lanka/Sinhala), gundumani/kundumani (in India/Tamil).It is a slender, perennial climber with long, pinnate-leafleted leaves that twines around trees, shrubs, and hedges.
